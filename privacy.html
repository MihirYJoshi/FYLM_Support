Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

FYLM ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username you choose. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Content You Create

We store the movie rankings, watchlist entries, notes, comments, and social interactions (likes, follows) that you create within the app. This is the core data that powers your personalized experience.

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as session timestamps and error logs to maintain app stability. We do not track granular analytics about individual in-app behavior beyond what is necessary for the service to function.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the FYLM ranking and social features
  • To calculate aggregated global rankings (using anonymized, aggregated scores)
  • To send in-app notifications for social interactions (likes, comments, follows, tags)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

Public Content

Your username, rankings, watchlist, and any notes you post are visible to other FYLM users — including users who visit your profile or see your posts in their feed. If you follow someone or they follow you, your activity will appear in their feed.

Service Providers

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider (database and authentication). Your data is stored on their platform subject to their security standards and privacy practices.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. Notification records are automatically cleared after 3 days. If you delete your account, your personal data (including rankings, notes, and social connections) is permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all of your rankings and activity directly within the app.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account from the Profile tab at any time.
  • Corrections: You can update your username or other account details from your profile settings.

To request a copy of your data or for any other privacy-related inquiry, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

FYLM is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of FYLM after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy? Reach out at support@fylmapp.com.
